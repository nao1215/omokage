# Lamplight

At night I turn the lights down low. There's no real reason. It's just that in the soft light, my thoughts come loose and settle slowly.

I open my notebook and write a little about the day. Some days the words don't come, but that's not so bad either. Looking at the lines is enough for me.

I'm not doing anything special. Even so, with a quiet hour like this, the end of the day feels a little gentler.
