# Walks

When it's clear out, I walk down by the river. I don't pick a route. I just turn wherever it's quiet and keep going.

The light shifts while I walk, and I stop more than I mean to. Sometimes I lose the thread of what I was thinking, and that's fine.

I come home without doing much. Still, my legs are tired in a good way, and the evening's a little softer for it.
