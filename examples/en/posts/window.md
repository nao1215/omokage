# The Window

I keep a chair by the window, and I sit there more than anywhere. There's no reason for it. It's just that watching the street go by lets my thoughts unwind.

I open a notebook and write a little about the day. Some days the words don't come, but that's not so bad. Looking at what I wrote is enough for me.

I'm not doing anything special. Even so, with a quiet hour like this, the day ends a little softer.
