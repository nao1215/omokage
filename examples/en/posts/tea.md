# Tea

In the afternoon I stop and make a pot of tea. I warm the cup first, the way someone showed me once, and wait for the leaves to open.

I don't measure anything. I pour, I wait, and I drink it while it's hot. The steam fogs the window, and watching it slows the afternoon down.

It's a small thing. But on a quiet day, that one warm cup is enough to keep me going.
