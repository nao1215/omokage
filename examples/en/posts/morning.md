# Mornings

Lately I've been getting up a little early. There's no real reason. It's just that when it's still quiet outside, my head settles down slowly.

First I boil some water and drink a cup of it plain. While it cools, I crack the window and let the air in. The cold comes through, and I wake up bit by bit.

I'm not doing anything special. Even so, these little habits make the day start softer.
